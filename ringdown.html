The Baltimore Ringdown

A Human Directory

This is a ringdown application that will allow you to call through a list of volunteers, in order to connect you with services in Baltimore City.
These are people just like you. People that have been there and understand what you are going through. We hope that you find this useful, and if you feel like you have something to give, please don't hesitate to volunteer!
