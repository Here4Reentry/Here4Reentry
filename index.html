About Our Work

This project is focused around creating a toolset for “returning citizens” to connect socially with one another, learn important information about quality social services, and organize politically to advocate for issues relevant to their lives and their communities. These are our neighbors, our friends and our family. All of these projects have been co-designed with Baltimore returning citizen leaders.
